thief


How does it feel to be a thief. To steal a heart that is locked away somewhere quiet, somewhere safe, somewhere secret, and yet a crime is not committed. How does it feel to walk free with something that was never yours, while I serve the sentence of remembering. Remembering. How did it feel to live unbothered, knowing you linger in my mind, like a broken record spinning endlessly, stuck on the same fragile line, repeating the same part of the song.
